GAME RULES

The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls dices as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his CURRENT score. But, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. And if he rolls two 6's in a row, he looses his ENTIRE score. In each way, after that, it's the next player's turn. The player can also always choose to HOLD, which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score but it's the next player's turn. The first player to reach 50 points on GLOBAL score wins the game (you can change the score to whichever you wish in the bracket 'FINAL SCORE' bellow).
