GAME RULES

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls dices as many times as he wishes.
  • Each result gets added to his CURRENT score.
    • But, If the player rolls 1, all his ROUND score gets lost.
    • If he rolls two 6's in a row, he looses his ENTIRE score.
    • In each way, after that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can also always choose to HOLD, which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score, but it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 50 points on GLOBAL score wins the game -- you can also change the score to whichever you wish in the bracket 'FINAL SCORE' bellow.
